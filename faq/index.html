Frequently asked questions

Is Halftone free?

Yes — completely free and open-source, with no ads, no sign-up, no watermark and no limits.

Are my images uploaded to a server?

No. Vectorizing runs entirely in your browser using WebAssembly, so your images never leave your device — nothing is uploaded anywhere.

Which image formats can I convert to SVG?

PNG, JPEG, WebP, GIF, BMP and AVIF, up to 25 MB. The output is a clean, scalable SVG.

What does vectorizing (tracing) an image do?

It converts a raster image made of pixels into a scalable SVG made of vector paths, so it stays sharp at any size — ideal for logos, icons and pixel art.

Can I control the result?

Yes — it is simple but complete. Choose a color palette (or black & white) and fine-tune smoothness, detail and contrast, and crop or rotate before you export.

Is it really private?

Yes. Everything runs client-side, there are no analytics trackers, and both your images and the resulting SVG stay on your machine.
