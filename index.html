Frequently asked questions

Is Halftone free?

Yes — completely free, with no ads, no sign-up, no watermark and no limits.

Are my images uploaded to a server?

No. Vectorizing runs entirely in your browser using WebAssembly, so your images never leave your device.

Which image formats can I convert to SVG?

PNG, JPEG, WebP, GIF, BMP and AVIF, up to 25 MB.

What does vectorizing (tracing) an image do?

It turns a raster image made of pixels into a scalable SVG made of vector paths, so it stays sharp at any size.

Can I control the result?

Yes — it is simple but complete. Choose a color palette (or black & white) and fine-tune smoothness, detail and contrast, and crop or rotate before you export.
